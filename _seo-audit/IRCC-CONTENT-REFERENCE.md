## EXPRESS ENTRY

**Express Entry (umbrella):** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry.html
Manages FSW, FST, CEC. Profile → CRS ranking → ITA → 60 days to apply. CRS criteria: core human capital, spouse, skill transferability, +600 for a provincial nomination. **2025: job-offer points removed from CRS. 2026: IRCC public consultation on reforming CRS + high-skilled programs; emphasis on in-Canada/CEC draws.**

**Federal Skilled Worker (FSW):** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/who-can-apply/federal-skilled-workers.html
≥1 yr continuous skilled work (NOC TEER 0/1/2/3) in last 10 yrs; **CLB 7**; ECA for foreign education; **must score ≥67/100** on six selection factors; proof of funds required.

**Federal Skilled Trades (FST):** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/who-can-apply/federal-skilled-trades.html
≥2 yrs (or 3,120 hrs) in a skilled trade in last 5 yrs; **CLB 5 speaking/listening, CLB 4 reading/writing**; need a valid job offer (≥1 yr) **OR** a certificate of qualification; proof of funds required.

**Canadian Experience Class (CEC):** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/who-can-apply/canadian-experience-class.html
≥1 yr skilled Canadian work (1,560 hrs) in last 3 yrs, while authorized; **CLB 7** (TEER 0/1) or **CLB 5** (TEER 2/3); **no proof of funds**. Most common in-Canada route; frequent 2025–26 draws.

**Category-Based Draws:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/rounds-invitations/category-based-selection.html
Invites by in-demand occupation/French even below general cut-off. **2026 categories: French-language proficiency (NCLC 7+), Healthcare & social services, Trades, Education.** (STEM & Agriculture dropped after 2025; Transport discontinued.) Occupation/NOC lists change yearly — verify live.

**Supporting:** Proof of funds (scales by family size; not for CEC) https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/documents/proof-funds.html · Language tests (IELTS General / CELPIP-General; TEF/TCF Canada; results <2 yrs) https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/documents/language-test.html · NOC 2021/TEER 0–5 https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/find-national-occupation-code.html · ECA (WES/IQAS/ICES/ICAS/CES/MCC; valid 5 yrs) https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/documents/education-assessment.html

**National hub:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/provincial-nominees.html · Enhanced (EE) process: `.../provincial-nominees/express-entry/eligibility.html` · Base process: `.../provincial-nominees/non-express-entry/eligibility.html`. **enhanced = +600 CRS; base = paper nomination + separate IRCC application.** *No per-province canada.ca pages — link each province's own site (below).* **2024 federal cut PNP allocations ~50%; 2025 partial top-ups; 2026 tight → stream closures.**

**Alberta — AAIP:** https://www.alberta.ca/alberta-advantage-immigration-program (updates: /aaip-updates)

Business: **Rural Entrepreneur, Graduate Entrepreneur, Foreign Graduate Entrepreneur, Farm**. Overhaul effective May 28 2026 ("Care, Build, Innovate"). Streams: **Skilled Worker** (+ EE BC), **Health Authority** (+ EE BC), **Entrepreneur Immigration** (Base / Regional / Strategic Projects). **CLOSED: International Graduate, International Post-Graduate, Entry-Level & Semi-Skilled (ELSS), tech draws** (2024–26) — info pages only.

**Saskatchewan — SINP:** https://www.saskatchewan.ca/residents/moving-to-saskatchewan/live-in-saskatchewan/by-immigrating/saskatchewan-immigrant-nominee-program
2026 sector "Talent Pathways." Streams: **International Skilled Worker – Employment Offer, Student pathway, Health Talent, Tech Talent, Agriculture Talent, Entrepreneur, Farm Owner/Operator**. **ISW Express Entry & Occupations-In-Demand sub-categories wound down for 2026** (now mostly base). **Manitoba — MPNP:** https://immigratemanitoba.com/
EOI points system. Streams: **Skilled Worker** (in-Manitoba SWM / Overseas SWO; SWO needs MB connection), **International Education** (Career Employment, Graduate Internship, Intl Student Entrepreneur Pilot), **Business Investor** (Entrepreneur / Farm Investor). **Ontario — OINP:** https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-immigrant-nominee-program-oinp (streams: /streams; 2026 updates: /2026-ontario-immigrant-nominee-program-updates)
**⚠️ Reg. 421/17 amended; framework effective MAY 30 2026 — all 9 legacy streams lose legal basis; replacement rules not yet published.** Legacy (still top search terms): Human Capital Priorities (enhanced), French-Speaking Skilled Worker (enhanced), Skilled Trades (enhanced), Masters Graduate, PhD Graduate, Employer Job Offer ×3 (Foreign Worker / Intl Student / In-Demand Skills), Entrepreneur. Proposed redesign: merge 3 Employer Job Offer → 1 (TEER 0–3 / 4–5 tracks); new Priority Healthcare, Exceptional Talent, revamped Entrepreneur; mandatory employer registration. **Write as forthcoming.**

**PEI PNP:** https://www.princeedwardisland.ca/en/topic/office-immigration
Labour Impact (Skilled Worker in PEI / Outside Canada, Critical Worker, International Graduate), Express Entry (enhanced), Business (Work Permit/Entrepreneur). **2025 allocation cut ~50% to 1,025; prioritizing healthcare/trades/childcare/manufacturing.** Also participates in AIP.

**Nova Scotia — NSNP:** https://liveinnovascotia.com/nova-scotia-nominee-program
**Consolidated 10→4 streams (new EOIs after Feb 18 2026):** Express Entry (enhanced; absorbed Labour Market Priorities + Physicians), Skilled Worker (base; absorbed Critical Construction Worker Pilot + Physician), Entrepreneur, International Graduate Entrepreneur. **No provincial application fee; 12-month EOI validity (May 1 2026).**

**New Brunswick — NBPNP/INB:** https://www2.gnb.ca/content/gnb/en/corporate/promo/immigration/immigrating-to-nb/nb-immigration-program-streams.html
Streams: Express Entry (enhanced), Skilled Worker, Strategic Initiative (French-aligned enhanced), Business Immigration, Critical Worker Pilot, Private Career College Graduate Pilot. **Controlled reopening from Feb 5 2025; reduced allocation. (NB's AIP separately paused Apr 2025.)**

**Newfoundland & Labrador — NLPNP:** https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration/immigrating-to-newfoundland-and-labrador/provincial-nominee-program/overview/
Streams: Express Entry Skilled Worker (enhanced), Skilled Worker, International Graduate, Priority Skills NL, International Entrepreneur, International Graduate Entrepreneur. **EOI model (Feb 19 2025); ALL provincial processing fees eliminated Dec 5 2025 ($0); ~25-day target.**

**Yukon — YNP:** https://yukon.ca/en/yukon-nominee-program
Employer-driven. Streams: Skilled Worker, Critical Impact Worker, Express Entry (enhanced), Community Program, Business Nominee. 2025 allocation 282.

**Northwest Territories — NTNP:** https://www.immigratenwt.ca/
Streams: Employer-Driven (Skilled Worker / Entry-Level-Semi-Skilled / NWT Express Entry-enhanced), Business, Francophone. 2025 intake capped 300.

## FAMILY SPONSORSHIP

Hub: https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/family-sponsorship.html

**Spouse/Common-Law/Conjugal:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/family-sponsorship/spouse-partner-children.html
Sponsor = citizen/PR/registered Indian, 18+. Usually **no income test**. **Spouse** (legally married) · **Common-law** (12 mo continuous cohabitation) · **Conjugal** (1 yr, barred from cohabiting/marrying; **outside Canada only**). **Inland** = SOWP available after AOR, must cohabit in Canada; **Outland** = mobility-friendly. **Undertaking 3 yrs. 5-year sponsorship bar** if you were yourself sponsored. **Conditional PR repealed 2017 — don't call it conditional.** Jan 2025 SOWP restrictions targeted spouses of temp workers/students, **not** family-class sponsorship. Quebec sponsors of partners & dependent children 18+ **paused to Jun 25 2026**.

**Dependent children:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/age-limit-requirements-dependent-children.html
**Under 22 & no spouse/partner**; 22+ if dependent due to a condition since before 22. Age **locked in at complete-application receipt**. No income test unless grandchildren involved.

**Parents & Grandparents (PGP):** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/family-sponsorship/sponsor-parents-grandparents.html
Lottery: submit **Interest to Sponsor**, IRCC invites. **MNI = LICO + 30% for each of 3 prior tax years** (CRA NOAs). **Undertaking 20 yrs (10 QC).** **2025 intake invited only from the 2020 interest pool (~17,860 invites, closed Oct 9 2025). 2026: NO new PGP applications. → Steer clients to Super Visa.**

**Super Visa:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/visit-canada/parent-grandparent-super-visa.html
Parents/grandparents; multiple entry up to 10 yrs; **stay up to 5 yrs/visit** (+2 yr extension). Host meets **LICO**; **medical insurance ≥1 yr, ≥$100,000**, health/hospital/repatriation; medical exam. **Jan 2025: foreign insurers accepted. Mar 31 2026: income-calc eased** (either of 2 prior tax years; visitor's income may count). Not PR.

**Other relatives / "lonely Canadian":** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/family-sponsorship/other-relatives/who-you-can-sponsor.html
Orphaned sibling/niece/nephew/grandchild (<18, single, both parents deceased); OR one relative of any age **only if** sponsor has no other living close relative/sponsorable relative. **Undertaking 10 yrs; LICO income.**

**Adoption:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/canadians/adopt-child-abroad/processes/choose-process/immigration.html — sponsorship route; genuine parent-child relationship; not for immigration purposes; provincial adoption authority approval.

## WORK PERMITS

**TFWP (LMIA) vs IMP (LMIA-exempt):** IMP hub https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/work-canada/hire-temporary-foreign/international-mobility-program.html
TFWP: employer needs **LMIA** (ESDC) → employer-specific permit; compliance fee $1,000. IMP: no LMIA (treaty/ICT/Francophone/IEC/PGWP/spousal OWP); Employer Portal + **$230** fee. **LMIA-exempt ≠ work-permit-exempt.** **Sept 26 2024 TFWP tightening: low-wage cap 20%→10% (health/construction/food keep 20%); no low-wage LMIA processing in CMAs with unemployment ≥6%; high-wage threshold raised ~20% over median; Oct 28 2024 accountant/lawyer attestations dropped.**

**IEC / Working Holiday:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/work-canada/iec.html — youth (18–35/30) from YMA countries; **Working Holiday (open), Young Professionals (employer-specific), Intl Co-op (internship)**; pool/round-based; 2026 pools opened Dec 2025.

**PGWP:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/study-canada/work/after-graduation/about.html — open permit; program ≥8 mo; apply within 180 days; **8 mo–3 yrs** (≥2-yr program → 3 yr; master's ≥Feb 15 2024 → 3 yr). **Nov 1 2024: language (CLB 7 university / CLB 5 college) + field-of-study for non-degree grads (degree grads exempt). Mar 2025: college bachelor's grads exempt from field rule.**

**BOWP:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/work-canada/pr-work-permits/bridging.html — open permit while awaiting PR decision; in Canada, valid status + valid work permit, principal applicant past AOR; eligible streams EE/most PNP/Quebec SW; up to 24 mo.

**Spousal/Family OWP:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/news/notices/changes-open-work-permits-family-members-temporary-residents.html — **Jan 21 2025 restrictions:** spouses of students qualify only if student in **master's ≥16 mo / doctoral / listed program**; spouses of workers only if worker in **TEER 0/1 or select TEER 2/3 shortage occupations** with **≥16 mo left**; **dependent children generally no longer eligible.**

**ICT:** IMP codes C61/C62/C63 — qualifying corporate relationship; ≥1 yr similar role in past 3 yrs; **Executive / Senior Manager / Specialized Knowledge**; up to 7 yrs (exec) / 5 yrs (specialized). Tightened specialized-knowledge scrutiny.

**Francophone Mobility:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/work-canada/special-instructions/francophone-mobility.html — LMIA-exempt (C16); **NCLC 5+**; job outside Quebec, any TEER (except primary agri TEER 4/5); strong overlooked route (2023 expansion to lower-skilled).

## CITIZENSHIP & PR STATUS

**Citizenship by grant:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/canadian-citizenship/become-canadian-citizen.html — PR status; **1,095 days physical presence in 5 yrs** (pre-PR days = ½, cap 365); **taxes filed 3 of 5 yrs**; **CLB 4 (ages 18–54)**; **test (ages 18–54)**; under-18 & 55+ exempt; prohibitions (criminality/removal). Physical Presence Calculator: https://eservices.cic.gc.ca/rescalc/resCalcStartNew.do

**Citizenship test:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/canadian-citizenship/test/study.html — **20 Q, pass 15/20 (75%)**, from *Discover Canada*; usually up to 3 attempts.

**Citizenship by descent — Bill C-3:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/canadian-citizenship/act-changes/rules-2025.html
**Use Bill C-3 (Royal Assent Nov 20 2025, in force Dec 15 2025) — NOT the dead Bill C-71.** First-generation limit removed. **Born abroad BEFORE Dec 15 2025 to a Canadian parent → automatic/retroactive (Lost Canadians) → get a citizenship certificate.** **On/after Dec 15 2025 to a born-abroad Canadian parent → only if parent has 1,095 days physical presence (substantial connection) before birth/adoption.** Interim-measure applicants needn't reapply.

**Proof of citizenship / dual:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/canadian-citizenship/proof-citizenship.html — certificate **$75**, secure paper doc; proves (doesn't grant) status; processing stretched (~10 mo in 2026 due to descent surge). Canada allows dual citizenship (no application/certificate; check other country's law; dual citizens need valid Canadian passport to fly to/from Canada).

**PR card renewal:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/new-immigrants/pr-card.html — **inside Canada only**; must meet residency obligation; valid 5 yrs; online ~28 days, paper 2–3 mo.

**PRTD:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/application/application-forms-guides/guide-5529-applying-permanent-resident-travel-document.html — for PRs **abroad** without valid card; must meet 730-day obligation or H&C; single-use counterfoil to return.

**Residency obligation:** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/new-immigrants/pr-card/understand-pr-status.html — **730 days physical presence per rolling 5 yrs**; exceptions (accompanying Canadian spouse abroad, full-time employment abroad by a Canadian business, etc.). Breach → loss of status/removal order. IAD appeal: **60 days (abroad) / 30 days (removal order inside Canada)**; H&C relief under IRPA s.67(1)(c).

## REGIONAL & PILOT PROGRAMS

**Atlantic Immigration Program (AIP):** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/atlantic-immigration.html — employer-driven PR for NB/NS/PEI/NL, **no LMIA**; designated employer + provincial endorsement; skilled-worker (TEER 0–4) or international-graduate streams; **CLB 5 (TEER 0–3) / 4 (TEER 4)**; mandatory settlement plan. **OPEN; NB paused ~Apr 2025; PEI restricted to healthcare/construction/manufacturing.**

**Rural Community Immigration Pilot (RCIP):** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/rural-franco-pilots/rural-immigration.html — **launched Jan 30 2025** (RNIP successor; RNIP closed). 14 communities (ON: North Bay, Sudbury, Timmins, Sault Ste. Marie, Thunder Bay +1; MB: Steinbach, Altona/Rhineland, Brandon; BC: West Kootenay, North Okanagan-Shuswap, Peace Liard; AB: Claresholm; SK: Moose Jaw; NS: Pictou County). Designated-employer job offer + community recommendation. **Verify exact community list/caps live.**

**Francophone Community Immigration Pilot (FCIP):** https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/rural-franco-pilots/franco-immigration.html — **launched Jan 30 2025**; French (NCLC 5) PR to 6 Francophone-minority communities outside Quebec (Acadian Peninsula NB; Sudbury/Timmins/Superior East ON; St-Pierre-Jolys MB; Kelowna BC).
