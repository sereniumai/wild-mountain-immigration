# PNP Deep-Dive — Expert Content Dossiers (all 11 provinces & territories)

**Current to May 30, 2026.** We do **all PNPs except Quebec** (Quebec runs its own CSQ system — we don't do it). This is the writer's expert source for ranking #1 on every PNP. Each province: system, live streams, 2026 allocation & changes, pages to build, and the must-state facts thin competitors get wrong.

> **Universal accuracy rules (every PNP page):**
> 1. **Distinguish base vs enhanced.** *Enhanced* = Express-Entry-aligned, nomination adds **+600 CRS**. *Base* = paper nomination → separate IRCC PR application. Only specific named EE streams are enhanced.
> 2. **Eligibility ≠ invitation.** Almost all PNPs are now EOI/points-ranked — meeting the bar doesn't guarantee an ITA. Say so.
> 3. **2025 federal allocation cut (~50%)** is the story behind every stream closure/sector restriction. 2026 allocations partly rebounded but stay tight.
> 4. **Nomination ≠ PR.** IRCC issues PR on a separate application.
> 5. **Date-stamp everything** ("accurate as of May 2026") + link the official source. Many figures (fees, cut-offs, occupation lists) change monthly — several below are flagged **[VERIFY]**.

## 1. ALBERTA — AAIP ⭐ HOME PROVINCE (maximum depth)

**System:** Worker Expression of Interest (**WEOI**, live Sept 30 2024); draws "as needed" (never promise a cadence); WEOI valid 1 yr. **Fees: $135 WEOI (new Apr 7 2026) + $1,500 worker application.** Business streams use separate EOI + Business Proposal + Net Worth Worksheet, scored on a Points Grid. **2026 allocation 6,403** (down from 9,750 in 2024). Priority sectors 2026: health care, tech, construction, manufacturing, aviation, agriculture, rural.

**Live streams:**
| Stream | Type | Who | Must-know |
|---|---|---|---|
| Alberta Opportunity Stream (AOS) | base | TFWs already working in AB on valid LMIA/exempt permit | CLB 5 (TEER 0–3)/CLB 4 (TEER 4–5); **NOC 33102 = CLB 7**; carries ~80% of invite volume |
| Alberta Express Entry Stream | **enhanced** | EE-pool candidates matching AB priorities | Hard floor **CRS 300**; **Family Connection + Occupation-in-Demand pathway discontinued Mar 17 2025** |
| → Accelerated Tech Pathway | enhanced | Tech workers w/ AB job | EE primary occupation must match the AB job |
| → Dedicated Health Care Pathway | enhanced (+ non-EE variant) | Health professionals w/ AB offer | Active draws; non-EE variant exists |
| → Law Enforcement Pathway | enhanced | Police/peace officers | Identified by AB Assoc. of Chiefs of Police |
| Rural Renewal Stream | base (community-endorsed) | Workers w/ rural job + community endorsement | **Overhauled Jan 1 2026:** endorsement valid 12 mo; **valid work permit required — implied/maintained status NOT accepted, restoration refused**; in-AB TEER 0–5 vs outside TEER 0–3; per-community caps |
| Tourism & Hospitality Stream | base | T&H workers w/ AB offer | Launched Mar 1 2024 |
| Rural / Graduate / Foreign Graduate Entrepreneur; Farm Stream | base (business) | Entrepreneurs/farmers | Net worth ~$300k, invest ~$100k (urban)/$50k (rural) **[VERIFY official points grid]**; Graduate Entrepreneur needs PGWP w/ ≥2 yrs remaining at EOI |

**Pages to build** (`/immigrate/provincial-nominee-program/alberta/`): hub · opportunity-stream · express-entry-stream (+ accelerated-tech, health-care, law-enforcement sub-pages) · rural-renewal-stream · tourism-hospitality-stream · rural-entrepreneur · graduate-entrepreneur · foreign-graduate-entrepreneur · farm-stream · **aaip-draws** (living page) · processing-times-fees. Target keywords = exact stream names.

**Expert must-states:** allocation 6,403 (down from 9,750); draws are ad-hoc; only EE Stream is enhanced; the $135 WEOI fee + dates; AOS = "already working in AB" (not from-abroad); kill any page still listing Family Connection / Occupation-in-Demand; Rural Renewal's new work-permit rule.

## 2. BRITISH COLUMBIA — BC PNP

**System:** **SIRS** points (max 200) registration → targeted ITAs. Two divisions: Skills Immigration + Entrepreneur. Health Authority & new Rural/Remote Health initiative skip SIRS (apply direct). EEBC option (Skilled Worker + Health Authority) = +600 CRS. **Fees: registration $0; Skills application $1,750 (up from $1,475, Jan 22 2026).** SIRS top factor = **hourly wage (55 pts)**; regional tilt (Metro Vancouver scores lowest). **2026 allocation 5,254**; target 35% outside Greater Vancouver.

**The "Care / Build / Innovate" overhaul** (announced Apr 23; new Skills Immigration Program Guide effective **May 28 2026**): 100% of nominations directed to **Care** (health/childcare/vet ~36 NOCs), **Build** (9 SkilledTradesBC-certified construction trades), **Innovate** (High Economic Impact: TEER 0–3 at high wage $59→$70/hr OR SIRS 135+).

**Live streams:** Skilled Worker (base + EEBC) · Health Authority (direct + EEBC) · Temporary Rural/Remote Health Support (NOC 64410/65310/65312; **registration opens Jun 15 2026**, ~250 spots) · Entrepreneur Immigration Base ($600k net worth/$200k invest/≥115 pts) · EI Regional ($300k/$100k/mandatory exploratory visit) · EI Strategic Projects (corporate).
**CLOSED (INFO pages):** International Graduate (Nov 2025), International Post-Graduate (Jan 2025), Entry-Level & Semi-Skilled/ELSS (Apr 2026), tech draws (Dec 2024).

**Pages to build** (`.../british-columbia/`): hub · skilled-worker · health-authority · rural-remote-health-support · care-build-innovate · **sirs-points-calculator** · express-entry-bc · entrepreneur-base · entrepreneur-regional · strategic-projects · draws-cut-off-scores · + 4 INFO/closed pages.

**Expert must-states:** Skills collapsed to Skilled Worker + Health Authority + temp Rural/Remote; ALL selection runs through Care/Build/Innovate (eligibility ≠ ITA); allocation only 5,254; cite the May 28 2026 guide; wage drives SIRS; closed streams' exact dates. **[VERIFY]** EI fees ($300/$3,500 are 2024 figures) + the 9 Build trades list.
**Official:** welcomebc.ca/immigrate-to-b-c/about-the-bc-provincial-nominee-program

## 6. PRINCE EDWARD ISLAND — PEI PNP
